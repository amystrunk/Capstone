Recent Reviews

Class of 2024

"Bellarmine University is a more freeing place than high school was, and offers many more opportunities to engage with other lgbtq+ students. We have various alliance groups and events tailored toward our community that were never present or possible in other settings, so it allows us to feel more included and a higher sense of belonging. Even with these, however, there is always room for improvement and more things available for us, and I hope we are able to keep moving in better directions."

Class of 2024

"Centre is much more inclusive than my high school and family. I feel like I can be myself on campus and am excited to participate in the campus Pride event."

Class of 2023

"Centre College is very inclusive and welcoming. We have a Pride Alliance which is student-led, and the faculty in the community are very visible. There is a Pride Space that is secured by a coded lock and is not widely advertised to the general campus community. Overall a great place to be!"
