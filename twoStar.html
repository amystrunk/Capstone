  • Events and Programming: Availability, quality, and frequency of convocations, presentations, Pride celebrations, courses and majors related to gender and sexual identity.

  • Student Facilitated: Organizations, groups, and clubs that build relationships and strengthen the LGBTQ+ student community.

  • Faculty and/or Staff Facilitated: Committees, organizations, groups, and clubs that foster collaboration and cooperation between faculty, staff, and students to celebration and strengthen the LGBTQ+ community on campus.
