About Us

Collegiate Peer Pride Review provides a platform for alumni, current, and prospective college students to share information. Alumni and current students are encouraged to rate their experiences with LGBTQ+ diversity, inclusion, and programming at the colleges and universities they attended to aid prospective students to make informed decisions without relying solely on the feedback of college administrators.

Going off to college can be an exciting, overwhelming, scary experience, but it is also an opportunity to find yourself, reinvent yourself ...or to show your true colors... Our goal is to help students find a college that aligns with their values, while providing a safe, understanding, inclusive environment.

Background

Collegiate Peer Pride Review began development in April 2022 as a capstone project for the Spring 2022 Commonwealth Coders boot camp. It was created to fulfill a gap our group recognized when some group members, as parents, were attempting to help their LGBTQ+ students research colleges. Students are encouraged to review school websites, tour campus, talk to administration, but realized there was not a resource to hear from current and previous students.

Commonwealth Coders Boot Camp

Commonwealth Coders is an intensive 16-week training course that prepares participants for a career as a Junior Web Developer. A collaborative effort between the South Central, Cumberlands, Bluegrass and Northern Kentucky Workforce Boards, this program provides tremendous and unique career opportunities, especially for those across the state interested in working remotely.
