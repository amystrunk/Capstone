Bellarmine University Reviews

Veritatis Amore – (In the love of truth.)

"We are an inclusive community that welcomes individuals from all walks of life and validates their uniqueness. We strive to connect and learn from one another so that we as a university can understand and foster mutual humility with those with whom we engage." (Diversity, Equity and Inclusion at Bellarmine)

Bellarmine University is a more freeing place than high school was, and offers many more opportunities to engage with other lgbtq+ students. we have various alliance groups and events tailored toward our community that were never present or possible in other settings, so it allows us to feel more included and a higher sense of belonging. even with these, however, there is always room for improvement and more things available for us, and i hope we are able to keep moving in better directions- Class of 2022

Bellarmine University hates gay people. We do not have sexual orientation or gender identity in our nondiscrimination policy, and professors love to out gay people to the rest of campus. I wish I hadn’t gone here, 0/10 do not recommend. - Class of 2018
